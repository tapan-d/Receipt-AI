# Product Vision

**Linear:** https://linear.app/ledger-ai/document/product-vision-a6b75546d3ce

Give users itemized control over expenses. Not just monthly totals — track individual items (e.g., olive oil price trend) across time.

## Core Problem

- We know roughly how much we spend on groceries but not the itemized breakdown.
- We can't track price of a specific item (e.g., olive oil) across months or years.
- Expense data is scattered: email receipts, paper receipts, photos, texts.
- Users have no single place to query their own spending in natural language.

## Solution

- Unified receipt store — every receipt, every item, searchable.
- AI-powered natural language queries: "How much did I spend on car expenses last month?"
- Auto-discovery agents sweep email, photo library, WhatsApp — no manual uploads needed.
- Groups for households, projects, events — shared receipt tracking.
- Reports: pick receipts + individual line items, export or share.

## Target Users

- Individuals who want itemized spending visibility.
- Households tracking shared expenses.
- Small teams / contractors tracking project expenses.

## Design Principles

- Frictionless — auto-discovery over manual upload.
- Swanky, intuitive UI.
- Give control back to the user.
